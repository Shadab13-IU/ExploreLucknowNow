Famous Food

Lucknow is famous for its delicious Awadhi cuisine. You must try:

Kebab

Lucknow, the capital city of the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, is renowned for its rich culinary heritage, particularly its exquisite Awadhi cuisine. One of the most famous dishes to emerge from this culinary tradition is Lucknowi kabab. Lucknowi kabab, also known as Awadhi kabab, is a delectable delicacy cherished for its aromatic spices, tender meat, and unparalleled flavor. The kababs are typically made with finely ground meat, often lamb or chicken, although beef or goat meat can also be used. The meat is marinated in a blend of yogurt and a plethora of spices, including but not limited to garlic, ginger, cumin, coriander, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and nutmeg. This marinade imparts a rich depth of flavor and tenderizes the meat, ensuring each bite is succulent and bursting with taste..

Biryani

The Rumi Darwaza (Gate) is an incredible structure which stands imposingly between Bara Imambara and Chota Imambara in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. Constructed in 1784 under the rule of Nawab Asaf-ud-Daula, it is a fine example of Awadhi architecture, or the Lucknow School of Architecture. It was a style adopted by the Nawabs of Awadh to preserve the Mughal architectural style while experimenting with newer styles and materials.
